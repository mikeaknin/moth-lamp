## Full description

The night is loud and the porch lamp is dying. You are a determined moth with a light blaster and a questionable life plan.

Fly through the **Moonlit Backyard**, the **Neon Alley**, and **The Final Porch Lamp**. Dodge stingers, pop speech-bubble projectiles from Heckler Flies, shut down Lamp Leeches before they drink your timer dry, and face the multi-phase **Porchlight Tyrant**.

Features:

- Urgent Lamp Life timer balanced with glow pickups  
- Dash, power-ups, combos, and readable enemy telegraphs  
- Touch controls built for phones + full keyboard support  
- Local high scores and accessibility options  
- Optional Base profile linking — never required to play  

Tone: stylish, energetic, a little chaotic, and proudly fictional. No pay-to-win, no tokens required, no surprise wallet popups.
